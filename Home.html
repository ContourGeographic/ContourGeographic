Get the most meaning out of soil survey data - then go deeper...

Soils in our region exhibit considerable variation in the field.  County level soil survey data attempts to characterize this variation, but in most cases can only offer an approximation of actual soil conditions on the ground.  These variations affect soil productivity, drainage, and land use options.  We offer hi-resolution aerial imagery, contour maps, and soil science consultation to assist in land use planning, wetland delineation, irrigation planning, and farm and forest management.
